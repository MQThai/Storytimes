What is Storytimes

A place for you to get your creativity going. You can make a prompt for others or pick a prompt and write a piece inspired by the prompt. Your piece can be anything you want: a poem, a story, etc. that others can read and leave comments and constructive criticism. You could also just sit back and enjoy.

Tips For A Good Prompt

  • should inspire and provide minimal direction
  • don't be too vague
  • don't be too restrictive

Tips For A Good Piece

  • have 30 words minimum
  • keep it within prompt direction
